1. What products do you sell?

We offer a wide range of technology devices including smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, laptops, accessories, audio devices, and more.



2. Are your products original and brand-new?

Yes. All products sold at Alpha Mobiles are 100% original, sealed, and come with full manufacturer warranty, unless stated otherwise.



3. Do you provide warranty?

Absolutely! Every device comes with an official brand warranty. Warranty duration varies depending on the manufacturer and product type.



4. Can I return or exchange my purchase?

Yes. We offer a return/exchange policy for eligible items within a specific period (usually 7–14 days). Items must be unused and in original packaging.
Check our Returns Policy page for full details.



5. Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we provide fast home delivery locally and shipping to many regions. Delivery times vary based on location.



6. Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number by email or SMS.



7. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery (if available), credit/debit cards, mobile wallet payments, and online banking.



8. Do you offer device trade-ins?

Contact our support for eligibility.
